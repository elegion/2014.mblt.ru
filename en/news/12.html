Richard Pidgeon's speech: "Where's the Money - The Global App Economy"

As promised we start posting video presentations from international mobile conference #MBLT14 that was held on May 23rd in Digital October. Let’s begin with the first speech of Richard Pidgeon from Distimo — globally recognised leader in app store market data and analytics. In his speach Richard talks about market trends, presents the global revenue distribution data from AppStore and Google Play by region, highlights Russian market features and describes competitor apps performance through the example of Spotify and Soundcloud.
